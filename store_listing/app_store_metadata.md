## Full Description

### App Store (iOS)

Reeder is a beautifully crafted RSS reader designed for people who love to read. Subscribe to your favorite blogs, news sites, and podcasts — all in one elegant, distraction-free app.

**Key Features:**

• **Unified Timeline** — All your subscriptions in one chronological feed
• **Beautiful Reading** — Clean article rendering with adjustable font size and line height
• **Bionic Reading** — Speed-read with the innovative Bionic Reading mode
• **Podcast Player** — Built-in audio player with chapters, speed control, and background playback
• **Video Support** — Watch video content inline without leaving the app
• **Smart Tags** — Organize articles with Later, Bookmarks, Favorites, and custom tags
• **Powerful Filters** — Create custom timelines based on keywords and content types
• **Full-Text Search** — Find any article instantly across all your subscriptions
• **Multiple Themes** — Light, Dark, OLED Black, and the unique Dark Light hybrid mode
• **Reader View** — Strip away clutter for a focused reading experience
• **Tablet Optimized** — Adaptive 2-column and 3-column layouts for iPad
• **OPML Import/Export** — Easily migrate your subscriptions
• **Privacy First** — All data stored locally on your device, no tracking, no accounts

Reeder respects your time and attention. No ads, no algorithms, no social features — just you and your content.
